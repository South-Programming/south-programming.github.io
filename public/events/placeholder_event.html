Spooky Jam

DESCRIPTION

For this Halloween, Programming Club is hosting a Spooky Jam! A jam is where you can submit any project you want, big or small, as long as it relates to the theme. In this jam, the theme is spooky or halloween. What will you make? A haunted calculator? An AI model which detects how scary someone is? A ghost which haunts your mouse around the screen? It's all up to you.

TIP

Try new things. Hands-on experiences like this are a great way to learn new skills, languages, and frameworks.

SUBMISSION

Submit by October 28th. You can either submit it by emailing us with the subject [PROJECT NAME] - Spooky Jam - [NAME], or in the discord channel #spooky-jam. When you submit, you could just provide us folder, zip, or a github repo link.
