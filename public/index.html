Who We Are

At Burnaby South Programming Club, we host biweekly meetings on Wednesdays @ E133. At these meetings you can talk with like-minded people with a shared passion in programming, learn about events and oppritunities, and more. We welcome people of all experience levels. We also host monthly jams, where you can build projects with a theme which you can improve your skills with.
